In Sophmore year I wrote an essay about things fall apart. I was captivated by the tragic world of Okonkwo as his reality is lost and taken by colinizers. The essay took around 4 weeks to complete and the only source on information was the book by Chinua Achebe. It was personally rewarding to write as I learned lots about the Ibo and greater Nigerian culture.
